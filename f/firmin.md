# Firmin

![Firmin](fir.jpg)

**Biography**: Andrianiaina Firmin, known as Firmin is an old malagasy singer. He lives in Antananarivo. He sometimes sings with other singers.

**Genre:** Tsapiky/Salegy/Slow.
