# Eddy lejao

![Eddy lejao's photo](eddy-lejao.jpg)

He is a famous singer from the south of Madagascar. He started to be famous in 2008 when he released a song entitled "Jalim-pitia".


**Genre:** Tsapiky and Gasigasy
